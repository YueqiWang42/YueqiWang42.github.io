Yueqi Wang

Ph.D. Student at the University of Pittsburgh

I am currenty a third-year Ph.D. student in Department of Computer Science at the University of Pittsburgh, advised by Dr. Xulong Tang. I received my B.E. degree from Sun Yat-sen University in 2022.

My research interests lie primarily in the area of computer architecture, with a focus on virtual memory for multi-GPU.
